In Dress to Impress, each round begins with players receiving a theme to dress up for. They have 360 seconds (6 minutes) to design their outfit, with a maximum of 18 items equipped at once. Players can increase this limit to 24 by purchasing the Increased Item Limit gamepass.

Once time runs out, the game freezes the players and cuts them into the runway, where they can use poses to showcase their outfits. Players then vote for each other by voting the player(s) between 1 to 5 stars per player. After voting concludes, the top three players are displayed on the podium for about 4 seconds before the round leaderboard appears, showing the total earned by all participants. Players also receive for completing a full round. This cycle repeats with a new theme each round. Progression in the game allows players to rank up from "New Model" to "Fashion Goddess," unlocking exclusive items at certain ranks
